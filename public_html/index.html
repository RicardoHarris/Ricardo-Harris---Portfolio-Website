About Me

My name is Ricardo Harris, I am learning computer science.

I am a student in the APP Academy at Pasadena Highschool. This is my first website and first project; my portfolio!

I was born March 10, 2000 in Glendale, California, USA. I spent my
childhood moving around from place to place in southern California
but I spent the majority of my childhood in Pasadena, CA. My first
experience with programming was in 8th grade Robotics class with
Mr.Crosby. There I had the help of his assistant Grant and my colleges
to learn how to and preform how to program NXT robots. My team won
second place in the annual robotics competition of PUSD (Pasadena Unified
School District). My school won every other trophy and medal.
Crosby was a very inspiring person, though he sugar coated nothing,
he opened my eyes to how this world works. I was taught that in
this world there are producers and consumers. And I wanted to be
a producer. Now I'm off to the right track by entering the APP
Academy teaching me more advanced coding tools than C language such
as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am a programmer, and I'm preparing
for the future. In this world there are thinkers and there are doers.
And I ask myself "Why not be both?"
